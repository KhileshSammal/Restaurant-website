Our Story

In a world where food often follows formulas, Customwale Foods was born from a different idea — that true luxury lies in personalization, not pre-sets. Founded by culinary obsessives who believe that flavor should be as individual as your fingerprint, Customwale is more than just a place to eat — it’s a place to design your plate, your way. We blend age-old culinary techniques with a bespoke approach. Whether it's the spice level of your biryani, the aroma of your handpicked herbs, or the texture of your pasta, every detail is yours to command. But we didn’t stop at customization. We sourced rare ingredients from across India and beyond — saffron from Kashmir, heirloom rice from Tamil Nadu, cold-pressed oils from artisanal farms. Every dish we serve is a collaboration between your taste and our craft. This isn’t fast food. It’s flavor, fine-tuned. It’s for those who don’t just eat — they curate.

Burger

Pasta

French Fries

Cold Drinks

Rolls
